Quick and Easy Recipe

This fruit cake recipe never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 3 medium-sized bananas,1 apple,4-5 strawberries and 2 kiwis peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar and vanilla.
  3. Toss the fruits in the sugar-vanilla mixture.
